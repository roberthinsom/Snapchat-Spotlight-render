What is Snapchat Spotlight?

Snapchat Spotlight is a feature that was introduced by Snapchat in late 2020 as part of the app's initiative to compete with platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. It allows users to create and share short video content—up to 60 seconds in length—that is curated and featured in a dedicated Spotlight feed. This content is easily discoverable by other users, providing creators a chance to go viral and reach a much larger audience than they would with regular snaps.

Unlike traditional snaps, which are sent directly to friends or posted to stories, Spotlight videos are visible to everyone using Snapchat. The feature aims to highlight user creativity and provide a platform where anyone can be discovered based on their content's popularity rather than their follower count.

How to Use Snapchat Spotlight

Using Snapchat Spotlight is simple and intuitive. Follow these steps to create and share your videos:

  1. Make a Video: Open the Snapchat app and tap the camera icon to start recording your video. Use Snapchat's wide range of editing tools, filters, lenses, and effects to make your video visually appealing and engaging.
  2. Select 'Spotlight': After recording your video, tap the “Send To” button. Among the available options, select “Spotlight” to have your video featured in the Spotlight feed.
  3. Add Captions and Hashtags: Enhance your video with an interesting caption and relevant hashtags. Hashtags make your video discoverable to users who are interested in similar content.
  4. Publish: Once you're satisfied with your video, tap 'Send' to publish it to Spotlight. Snapchat’s algorithm will analyze its performance, including likes, shares, and engagement, to determine its reach.
  5. Track Your Performance: Keep an eye on how your video is performing by checking its likes, shares, and views. Additionally, if your video goes viral, you might earn money through Snapchat's monetization system.

How to Download & Share Snapchat Spotlight Videos

If you want to download or share a Spotlight video you’ve created, follow these steps:

  1. Open Snapchat and Go to Your Profile: Tap your profile icon in the top-left corner of the main screen to access your profile page.
  2. Access Spotlight & Snap Map: Scroll to the bottom of your profile page to find the Spotlight & Snap Map section, where all your uploaded Spotlight videos are located.
  3. Select the Video You Want to Download: Tap on the Spotlight section, and scroll down to select the video you wish to download to your device.
  4. Save the Video: After selecting your video, tap the three dots in the top-right corner of the screen to open a menu. Choose “Save Snap” to download the video directly to your device’s gallery.
  5. Share Your Spotlight Snaps: You can share your downloaded Spotlight video directly from your device’s gallery or use Snapchat’s share feature to send it to friends or post it on other social platforms.

Benefits of Using Snapchat Spotlight

Snapchat Spotlight offers a host of benefits for users, whether they’re seasoned influencers or just getting started:

  • Audience Reach: Spotlight enables you to reach a broader audience, even if you don’t have many followers. With the right content and hashtags, your videos can go viral and gain millions of views.
  • Monetization Potential: One of the most attractive aspects of Spotlight is its potential for monetization. If your video goes viral, you can earn money based on its performance, encouraging content creators to produce high-quality, engaging material.
  • Creative Freedom: Snapchat offers a wide range of filters, lenses, and editing tools, giving you the freedom to experiment with different styles and formats. Whether you want to create funny skits, tutorials, or artistic videos, the possibilities are endless.
  • Viral Opportunities: Since Spotlight videos are curated and amplified based on engagement, creators have a unique opportunity to go viral and gain significant exposure.

How to Post on Snapchat Spotlight

Posting to Snapchat Spotlight is quick and easy. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Create Your Video: Record a 60-second video using Snapchat’s camera and editing tools.
  2. Tap 'Send' and Select 'Spotlight': After recording, tap the “Send” button and choose “Spotlight” to share your video with the broader Snapchat community.
  3. Add Hashtags and Captions: Enhance your video with relevant hashtags and an engaging caption to increase its discoverability.
  4. Submit Your Video: Submit your video to Snapchat, where it will be reviewed and published based on Snapchat’s community guidelines.

How Snapchat Spotlight Algorithm Works

The Spotlight algorithm recommends content based on various factors, such as:

  • Likes and Shares: Videos that receive more likes and shares are more likely to be featured prominently in the Spotlight feed.
  • Watch Time and Skips: Snapchat also takes into account how long users watch a video and whether they skip it. Engaging content that keeps users watching to the end is more likely to be recommended.
  • User Interests: The more you engage with certain types of content, the more Snapchat will show you similar videos in your Spotlight feed.
